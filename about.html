About Me

J.H. Freedman is a mixed media artist with a background in animation. Throughout the mediums he works in, there is a bleak atmosphere with a darkly comic undertone. He finds absurdity in the life we take for granted and enjoys satirizing the corporate world.

He utilizes magic and science fiction in his art as tools to explore sociopolitcal situations and the characters that would arise from them. It gives him the reins for unlimited creative expression and the challenge of realistic expectations.
