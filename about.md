I'm Jake, a multimedia expressionist from Troy, NY. This site acts as a collection of my works, from the audiovisual to the static, from the representational to the abstract. 

* The [gallery](/gallery/index.html) contains my charcoal, pastel, and digital ink compositions. 

* The [theater](/theater/index.html) contains my charcoal and pastel animations on paper. 

* The [jukebox](/jukebox/index.html) contains my bass guitar music. 

* The [library](/library/index.html) contains articles and my blog. 

I add stuff consistently but not frequently, so I encourage you to check in every couple weeks ~~or months~~ to check for [updates](/library/updates.html).
